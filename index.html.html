Vacuum Repair — All Brands, All Ages

Free estimates · Honest pricing · Most repairs completed in 1–3 days · We fix what others won't touch

Before You Buy a New One — Bring It to Us

Vacuums get thrown away every day for problems that cost $30 to fix. A worn belt, a clogged motor, a cracked hose, or a failed capacitor — these are all straightforward repairs that restore your vacuum to full performance.

We repair all makes and models, including vintage and discontinued machines that other shops refuse to touch. Our technicians have decades of hands-on experience and we maintain an extensive stock of parts — including hard-to-find components for older models.

Old vacuum models are a speciality. Have a machine from the 70s, 80s, or 90s? We can often source or fabricate the parts needed to get it running again. We've repaired Kirbys, Filter Queens, Eluxes, and machines people have had for 40+ years.

Our Story

Three Decades of Trusted Local Service

Mississauga Vacuum Centre has been part of the Clarkson community since the 1990s. What started as a local shop focused on doing honest work has grown into one of the most trusted vacuum specialists in the region — but we've stayed true to what made us who we are.

Our store is owned and operated by Nader Hussien, who has built the business around a simple principle: if someone brings us a vacuum, we take care of it like it's our own. That means straight answers, fair prices, and never recommending a repair or purchase that isn't in the customer's best interest.

We are an authorized Miele dealer and an authorized dealer for Canada Vac and Cyclo Vac central vacuum systems. We partner with home builders across the GTA to install central vacuum systems in new residential developments. We carry parts for virtually every vacuum brand, including discontinued and vintage models that other shops won't touch.

Our shop is at 1375 Southdown Road in the Clarkson area — easy to find, easy to park, and staffed by people who genuinely love what they do.
